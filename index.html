What is Maedx?

Maedx is a modern learning platform built for architects, engineers, and sustainability professionals who want to design smarter and lead with data‑driven insight. Our content is based on real project experience, rigorous QA/QC workflows, and clear communication.

What You Will Learn

Energy Modeling: Build accurate whole‑building models from concept to construction.

Net Zero Design: Understand strategies and performance metrics for low‑carbon buildings.

Digital Twins: Explore how data and simulation shape the future of building performance.
